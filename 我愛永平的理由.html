    Why I love Yongping

我是張永仙，今年46歲。我愛永平野生菌。永平環境好，隨時能吃到可口的菌類食物。

My name is Zhang Yongxian, 46 years old. I love Yongping because we have lots of fungus. The environment here is good and we can have delicious fungus at any time.

我是萬余成，今年12歲。我愛永平。我愛永平岑秀公園，也愛吃美食黃燜雞。

My name is Wan Yucheng and I am 12. I love Yongping,because I like Qinxiu Park very much. I also a fan of yellow-stewed chicken.

I am Dong Shiqi. I am 50 years old. I love Yongping. Yongpingers are simple and hospitality. It is always a pleasure to greet a friend from afar!

I am Huang Yingtuan, 65. I love Yongping. Yongping has beautiful scenery. I visit local temples a lot and God listen to me and response.
