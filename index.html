The three reasons why you should visit Yongping

Stepping on the ancient road, experiencing the disappearing caravan culture

中國最大回族村 感受民族融合

China's largest Hui village, feel the national integration

回歸自然 讓原始森林洗滌心靈

Getting back to nature, spend mindful time in the woods

位置及交通
Location and transportation

D永平縣城距離大理機場130公里，經320國道，大保高速，車程大約2個半小時，是大理的「西大門」。縣名源自於東漢年號。博南古道在境內綿延100多公里。

Yongping County is 130 kilometers from Dali Airport. The journey takes about two and a half hours though 320 Express and Dali-Baoshan Express. It is the “West Gate” of Dali. The county name is derived from the Eastern Han Dynasty. The Bonan Trail stretches over 100 kilometers in the territory.

氣候
Weather

海拔高低差異大，溫差大。全年平均氣溫15.8攝氏度。旅行四季皆可。

The difference in altitude is large and the temperature difference is large. The annual average temperature is 15.8 degrees Celsius. Travel is available all seasons.

飲食
Food

濃郁的馬幫飲食文化，配合當地食材。龐大的回族居民，提供富有特色，同時融合了漢族飲食文化的穆斯林食品。

A rich caravan food culture with local ingredients. Large Hui residents provide Muslim food with distinctive characteristics and a blend of Han food culture.

永平人
People

有22個民族聚居，截止2017年底，人口21萬。在這裏，可以看到不同民族保留著各自的文化特色，包括傳統節日，宗教，建築和飲食文化等。

There are 22 ethnic groups living in the area, with a population of 210,000 by the end of 2017. Here, you can see that different ethnic groups retain their own cultural characteristics, including traditional festivals, religion, architecture and food culture.
