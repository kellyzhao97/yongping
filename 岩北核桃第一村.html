    Why I love Yongping

永平核桃

Yongping Walnut

作為遊客來到永平，如果你問，一定要買哪些手信，那就一定不能錯過永平的“大”泡核桃。古人稱泡核桃為"神之果"，當代人將泡核桃稱為"營養寶庫"。是饋贈親友賓朋和居家食用的傳統風味食品。

As a visitor to Yongping, if you ask, what are must buy for your trip, we highly recommend Yongping's "big" walnut. The ancients called the walnuts "the fruit of the gods", and now people called the walnuts a "treasure of nutrition." It is not only the good choice gift for friends and relatives, but also traditional taste for you to enjoy.

很多地方都出產核桃，但是永平的這些核桃，有著和其他地方不一樣的地方。

Many places produce walnuts, but these walnuts in Yongping are special.

首先永平的核桃種植有三千五百多年的歷史，而且永平是雲南省最大的核桃交易所，被稱之為中國核桃集散地。

First of all, Yongping's walnut plantation has a history of more than 3,500 years, and Yongping is the largest walnut exchange market in Yunnan Province, known as the Chinese walnut distribution center.

其次永平縣出產的泡核桃，有果大、皮薄、仁白、味香，出油率高及營養豐富等特點，這是因為泡核桃在海拔1600米-2400米的適宜區域種植。

Secondly, Yongping’s walnuts are super large with very thin skin They also have white nut with strong flavor, oily and nutrient-rich. This is because these walnuts are planted in a suitable area at an altitude of 1600 m-2400 m.
